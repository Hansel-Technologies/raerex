About Us

Raerex East Africa prides itself in being one of the best Air-Conditioning, Refrigeration, Mechanical Ventilation Company in East African. Our headquarters is in Nairobi along with other subsequent branches in Kisumu, Mombasa, Uganda and Kigali. Raerex has been running for the past 25 years and has developed a good relationship with it's suppliers allowing the company to gain access to cutting age technology in the HVAC world. This has allowed us to work with prominent clientele which has provided an opportunity to constantly improve on our customer service and installation practices. Through this we emerged among the top 100 in the KPMG Top 100 Mid-Sized companies 2013/2014. We are registered under the National Construction Authority (N.C.A) category 1, Ministry of Public Works & Housing to undertake construction projects in both domestic, private & public institutions.
